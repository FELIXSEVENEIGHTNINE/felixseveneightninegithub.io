Home Tour: Living Room

This luxurious living room features built-in cabinets and tile flooring. Two huge picture windows and a sliding glass door provide stunning sunset views of the White Tank Mountains. A fireplace with art deco mantel is a focal point in the 20' x 14' room. This versatile room is perfect for quiet family evenings or larger get-togethers with friends.
