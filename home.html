ABOUT US

Real Living Real Estate is a full-service real estate brokerage franchise company with a comprehensive andintegrated suite of resources for franchisees and their sales professionals, as well as for consumers who work with them. Real Living leads the real estate industry with a customer satisfaction rating of 98 percent, based upon research conducted by a third party, independent research firm*.

Based in Minneapolis, Minn., HomeServices of America, Inc. is the second-largest, full-service residential real estate brokerage firm and the largest brokerage-ownedsettlement services provider in the U.S. and is the majority owner in the Prudential Real Estate and Real Living Real Estate franchise networks. Owned by MidAmerican Energy Holdings Company, a consolidated subsidiary of Berkshire Hathaway Inc., HomeServices' operating companies offer integrated real estate services, including brokerage services, mortgage originations, title and closing services, property and casualty insurance, home warranties and other homeownership services.
